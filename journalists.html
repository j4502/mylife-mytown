JOURNALISTS

  • Erin Dismeier

    Erin Dismeier was a student newscaster for KBIA in 2013. She has covered a wide array of regional news from Missouri Congressional bills to local government actions.

  • Naveen Mahadevan

    Naveen Mahadevan is a freelance photojournalist now based in Chennai.He specializes in studio and documentary portraiture. Mahadevan earned his masters degree in photojournalism from the Missouri School of Journalism.

  • Kevin Cook

    Kevin Cook won first and second places in Interpretive Project and Feature categories at College Photographer of the Year in 2013. Cook recieved his masters degree in photojournalism from the MU School of Journalism.

  • Benjamin Hoste

    Benjamin Hoste earned his masters degree from the MU School of Journalism. Hoste said his work is often driven by an interest in identity and how it interrelates to a given geography.Hoste is now a non-fiction, documentary photographer based in Brooklyn, NY.

  • Emily Kaiser

    Emily Kaiser studied journalism, international studies and spanish at the University of Missouri.

  • Yi-Chin Lee

    Yi-Chin Lee graduated from the Missouri School of Journalism in 2014 with a masters degree in photojournalism. Lee said in the program he learned to capture moments that tell stories with his eyes and his camera.
